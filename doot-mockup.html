Three steps

How Doot works

1

Pick or make a game

Grab one from Explore or build your own with the editor. Choose a theme that styles the lobby and the whole game.

2

Put it on the big screen

Open it on a TV, a projector, or a shared laptop. A join code and QR appear for the room.

3

Everyone plays along

The crowd joins from their phones, answers each round, and the results pop on screen. No app, no sign-in to play.
